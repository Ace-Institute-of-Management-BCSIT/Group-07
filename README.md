#Group 7

### 1. Blood Donation Finder
In critical medical emergencies, every second counts. This module enables users to locate and connect with blood donors instantly.
* **Location-Based Search:** Find specific blood types available in your immediate vicinity.
* **Time-Critical Alerts:** Streamlines the process of finding a match, saving precious time during life-threatening situations.
* **Direct Connectivity:** Facilitates quick communication between seekers and donors.

### 2. Professional On-Demand (Healthcare & Beyond)
This module brings expert care directly to the user's doorstep, ensuring professional intervention when it's needed most.
* **Medical Home Visits:** Request a doctor or nurse for home visits to assess the severity of a condition and provide essential first aid.
* **Scalable Service Model:** While launching with healthcare, the architecture is designed to scale for any professional service, including:
    * **Education:** Personal tutors and teachers.
    * **Maintenance:** Plumbers, electricians, and technicians.
    * **Domestic Help:** Cooks, cleaners, and caregivers.

### 3. Vehicle Rental System
A flexible transportation solution that caters to both short-term needs and planned journeys.
* **Custom Selection:** Users can rent their desired vehicle type based on specific requirements (size, luxury, or utility).
* **Flexible Scheduling:** Rent for a specific duration or a specific destination.
* **Seamless Booking:** Integrated booking flow for quick vehicle acquisition.
